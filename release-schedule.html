StoreCore Release Schedule


Release cycle

To allow for continuous development and predictable deployment, updates of the StoreCore e-commerce framework are released on the last Tuesday of each month. The current release schedule for all major and minor releases of StoreCore version 1 is outlined in the table below. In many retail operations, online orders that come in during the weekend, are processed and shipped on Monday. Therefore updates of software critical to key operations should be postponed to a Tuesday. Updating on Tuesday furthermore allows for the discovery of new features, and possibly bugs, over a three-day period during the remainder of the working week.
